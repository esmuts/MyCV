Hello. I'm Eckard. I live in Cape Town, and I love it here. I work as a teacher and academic researcher in the field of literature. I'm passionate about books and writing, and count myself lucky to be able to make a living sharing this passion with others.

I'm also a freelance writer and editor. From journalism and case studies to technical manuals and academic theses, I've (mostly) done it all. I'm good at translating knowledge work into compelling narrative content, and when I can find the time, I love writing book reviews.

More recently, I've started learning how to code. Computers and technology have always interested me, so during the pandemic I decided to take the plunge and sign up for a full-stack web development course. So far, so good! If you have any tips for a budding programmer, let me know...
